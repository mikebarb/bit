Policy on Google Sheets Use

This applicaiton - Big Improvements Tutoring Scheduler - does not read your data.

This application populates a google sheet / spreadsheet that you supply.

The process is that you create a spreadsheet that you wish this applicaiton to populate.

Then share this spreadsheet with 'mike@mikebarb.net'

Then add the url for this spreadsheet into you preferences.

The application will then populate this spreadsheet with the roster details upon your request.

You are then responsible for the distribution this information as required by your business.
